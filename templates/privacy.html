Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or any financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the expense tracking features of Spendly. We use your email address to identify your account and, if applicable, to send essential service notifications. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect it from unauthorized access. However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third-party services. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and do not have access to your data beyond what is necessary to run the service.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing any concerns promptly and transparently.
